Product description

Sad Emojis for when you don't feel like smiling!

Have you ever felt down? Too sad to talk? Too sad to type? Let our cute emojis communicate those deep emotions that are hard to put into words. -Emoji World

-=[ APP FEATURES ]=-
- Launch Sale 50% Off!
- Unique HD Sad Emojis!
- Super Fast Loading!
- Supports Facebook Messenger, WeChat, WhatsApp, LINE, Kakao Talk, Twitter, Kik, GO SMS, Google Messenger, Yahoo, Handcent, BBM, MMS, SMS, Email And More!

-=[ SAD EMOJIS HOW TO ]=-
- Method 1 -
To share a sad emoji, simply touch it, then touch the chat app you want to share it with!

- Method 2 -
Using your favorite chat app, go to attach an image. Choose Sad Emojis then pick a sad emoji to share!


Special Note:
If you know someone who is sending you a lot of our sad emojis, check on them and make sure they are okay. Because life is hard sometimes and we all need someone to lean on. -Emoji World
